Fried Eggplant and Tomatoes

At this time I have no photo of this dish nor can I find one online as it is a family recipie

Description

This is one of those family recipies where there are not always exact measurements. The dish consists of fried eggplants and pan fired tomamtoes. The tomatoes I personally have not mastered and am still working ont the recipie. This can be a little time consuming but well worth it in the end.

Ingridiants

  • Fresh Eggplant
  • Fresh Tomato
  • Lots of Butter
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Frying Oil

Steps

  1. Slice both eggplants and tomatoes into discs
  2. Prepare an egg wash with seperate plates of flour and bread crumbs
  3. Add frying oil into pan and heat it up
  4. Dip eggplant discs into egg wash and into the bread crumbs
  5. Fry eggplants in oil
  6. Start heading up a seperate pan with 1/2 stick of butter
  7. Dip tomato discs into egg wash and dredge in flour
  8. Add floured tomato into the pan with butter until no more tomamtoes can fit
  9. Scramble tomatoes almost like you would an egg
  10. season with salt and pepper to taste
