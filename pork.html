Stuffed Pork Chops

Description

Exceptionally moist and stuffed with a mixture of herbs and breadcrumbs that reminds me of Thanksgiving stuffing in the best possible way, this homey recipe will have you asking yourself why you donâ€™t cook pork chops more often.

Ingredients

  • 4 Pork Chops, 1" Thick
  • 1 Box Stove Top Stuffing
  • 3 Eggs Beaten
  • 1/2 Cup Flower
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Prepare stuffing mix according to package instructions.
  3. Scramble the eggs and add sea salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste. Place in a shallow dish.
  4. Place flour in another shallow dish.
  5. Place breadcrumbs in another shallow dish. Add garlic powder and combine well.
  6. Slice pork chops down the center to create a pocket, taking care not to slice them the whole way through.
  7. Stuff the chops with stuffing mix.
  8. Heat olive oil in a small pan over medium heat until it sizzles if anything is put into it.
  9. Dip chops in flour, then eggs, then breadcrumb mixture.
  10. Brown chops on all sides to get breading to stick to chops and stay crisp while baking.
  11. Transfer chops from pan to glass baking dish.
  12. Cover and bake 35-40 minutes.
